Hiring on Djinni:

Why Djinni?

Djinni gives you the opportunity to passive and anonymous search - when recruiters are looking for you. This reflects the realities of the labor market, where experienced professionals are in constant shortage.

How many offers will I get?

It strongly depends on your level, salary expectations and the city. Every week more than 15 thousand propositions pass through Djinni.

Can my employer find me at Djinni?

No. All profiles are anonymous. You can decline a job without revealing your identity.
